About Me

Full-funnel digital marketer. Data obsessive. Based in Austin, TX.

I'm TJ Marek — a full-funnel digital marketing strategist who connects paid media, SEO, CRO, and web design into a single growth system.

My approach starts at the bottom of the funnel and works backward. Before touching a campaign, I look at the attribution model, the conversion infrastructure, and where the actual friction is. Strategy without measurement is just opinion.

Over 5+ years I've managed millions in ad spend across Google, Meta, and organic channels — always with clean tracking, defensible numbers, and a clear line from campaign to revenue.

Based in Austin, TX. Open to remote projects, agency partnerships, and full-time roles in high-growth environments.
