About Me

The Strategist Behind the Numbers

I'm TJ Marek — a full-funnel digital marketing strategist based in Austin, TX. I connect the dots between brand awareness and closed revenue across every channel I touch.

My approach is data-first, always. Before recommending anything, I look at the tracking infrastructure, the attribution model, and where the real friction is in the funnel. Strategy without measurement is just opinion.

I specialize in building growth systems that scale — not one-off campaigns. That means clean GA4 setups, tight GTM implementations, paid media that feeds clean data into the algorithm, and landing pages that convert.

When I'm not in the accounts, I'm studying what's next — algorithm shifts, GEO tactics, auction dynamics, and the ever-moving landscape of digital marketing in 2026.
